About me

Cameron Robert Powell was born in Buffalo, New York. He taught himself to read, learned to play chess at the age of 3, and finished 8th-grade level mathematics while in the 3rd grade. With the intent from a young age to study animation, he enrolled the Digital Media Arts program at Canisius College on a full scholarship for academic excellence. On discovering a multitude of other interests, he focused his studies on music education, computer science & engineering, and finally geology. He graduated from SUNY Fredonia in 2012, and went on to win a number of awards from NASA while studying craters on Mercury while in Fairbanks, Alaska.

After visiting a friend in Iceland in 2015, Cameron decided to enroll in the graduate school at the University of Iceland, obtaining a master's degree in geology with a specialization in renewable energy. His final thesis heavily involved statistical analysis and scripting (mostly in Python).

When he's not seen catching Pokémon around Reykjavík while 10k run training, Cameron can be found writing and recording music, taking online courses to further his programming skills, and keeping apprised of current trends in video game marketing to aid his community management for the War of Omens digital CCG. He spends his remaining free time playing Dungeons & Dragons with a local gaming friend group, as well as making regular guest appearances on the official streamed D&D game Dice, Camera, Action! (featuring Chris Perkins as DM).
